    So what is a Landing Page

    And its differences from a regular site?

    • LANDING PAGE is a landing page on the Internet whose task is to provoke a visitor to perform a simple action: leave a request for a product or service, or call the indicated phone numbers with a specific question.
    • Landing Page is a powerful, effective marketing move, which should not be underestimated, even if you have a successful online store or information resource.

    Differences from the site:

    • Focused on a specific product or service
    • There are no distractions from the fulfillment of the main goal: leave a request or contact details
    • Correct and comprehensive presentation of information
    • Make a visitor buy your product with a probability of up to 50% more often from a regular site
